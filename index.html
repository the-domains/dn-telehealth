Survey: 75% of Providers Still in Early Stages of Telehealth

A KPMG survey found that a quarter of healthcare providers surveyed found that their virtual care programs - telehealth and telemedicine - were sustainable, while 35 percent of providers have not yet embarked on their programs. The poll reflected responses from 120 individuals, who identified themselves as working for healthcare providers, during a webcast on April 14, which was titled " Virtual Care - Establishing a Scalable and Sustainable Program as a Growth Strategy."
